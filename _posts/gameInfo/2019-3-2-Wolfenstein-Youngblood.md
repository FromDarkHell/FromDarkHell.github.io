## Mission Flow

1. General Winkler (M)
2. Riverside (M)
  - Rescue Marianne (S)
  - The Silent Outpost (S)
  - Chemical Warfare (S)
3. Raid - Brother 1 (M)
4. Raid - Brother 2 (M)
5. Raid - Brother 3 (M)
6. Raid - Lab X (M)
7. Siegturm (M)  

| Key    | Value          |
|--------|----------------|
| M      | Story Mission  |
| S      | Side Quest     |
| Number | Required Level |

Notes:
*  Brother 1, 2, 3 are all interchangable along with the missions that unlock their Underground.
